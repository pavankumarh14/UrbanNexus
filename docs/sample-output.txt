THE DIFFERENCE — Why clustering matters
======================================

                   Without Clustering    With Clustering
                   ------------------    ---------------
  Work orders:           5                     1
  Crews dispatched:      up to 5           1 emergency crew
  Citizen responses:     5 different       1 consistent message
  Root cause found:      NO                YES (burst main)
  Resolution time:       hours per ticket  single coordinated fix

This is the standout feature of UrbanNexus.
Batch 2 is specifically designed to trigger this.
Ingest Batch 1 first (no clusters), then Batch 2 (cluster fires).
